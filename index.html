Cryptocurrency Knowledge Hub

Bitcoin (BTC)

The first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009. Bitcoin operates on a decentralized network using blockchain technology.

  • Market Cap Leader
  • Store of Value
  • Limited Supply: 21M

Ethereum (ETH)

A decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and DApps. Ethereum introduced programmable blockchain technology to the world.

  • Smart Contracts
  • DeFi Leader
  • NFT Platform

Binance Coin (BNB)

The native cryptocurrency of Binance Chain and Binance Smart Chain. Powers the Binance ecosystem and its various applications.

  • Exchange Token
  • Smart Chain
  • Low Fees

Dogecoin (DOGE)

Started as a meme cryptocurrency, Dogecoin has grown into a popular digital currency for tipping and transactions, known for its fun community.

  • Community Driven
  • Fast Transactions
  • Low Transaction Fees

Avalanche (AVAX)

A high-performance blockchain platform focused on speed, low costs, and eco-friendliness. Supports multiple virtual machines including EVM.

  • High Speed
  • Eco-Friendly
  • Subnet Architecture

Ripple (XRP)

A digital payment protocol and cryptocurrency designed for fast, low-cost international money transfers and financial institution settlements.

  • Fast Settlements
  • Bank Partnerships
  • Cross-border Payments

Tether (USDT)

The most widely used stablecoin, pegged to the US Dollar. Tether provides stability in crypto markets and serves as a bridge between traditional finance and crypto.

  • 1:1 USD Peg
  • High Liquidity
  • Multi-chain Support

Solana (SOL)

A high-performance blockchain platform known for its incredible speed and low transaction costs. Popular for DeFi, NFTs, and Web3 applications.

  • Ultra-fast Transactions
  • Proof of History
  • Scalable Architecture
